Roadbud is a mobile application that combines all relevant information about your local roads and presents it to you in a digestable way. Stay informed and stay safe.

FEATURES

CDOT Alerts

Access live data from CDOT about closures, accidents, planned events and more.

Live Video

See CCTV video in real time so you can scan the roads before you go.

Custom Alerts

Set up custom alerts to get notified when something happens on a road you use frequently.

Community

See posts from other users in your community and keep eachother informed.

Trip Planner

Find the best available route and get the most accurate ETA information based on multiple sources.
